Last Updated: February 9, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Android application.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in "privacy by design." We only collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide and improve our services:

  • Authentication Data: When you sign in, we collect your email address. This is used solely to identify you as a user and manage your account security.
  • Anonymous Analytics: We collect anonymous usage data (such as feature usage, crash reports, and device type). This information does not contain any personally identifiable information (PII) and cannot be linked back to you.

2. How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the service (authentication).
  • To monitor the performance of the app and fix technical issues.
  • To understand aggregate user trends and improve the overall experience.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your email address or usage data to outside parties. Your email address is strictly used for the core functionality of the app.

4. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your email address during transmission and storage. However, please remember that no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics for Firebase) to help us understand app usage. These services collect data anonymously. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.
